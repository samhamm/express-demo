BusMall Marketing Study

Thank you for participating in BusMall's product study. Your input will help us to tailor our catalog experience for the maximum enjoyment of numerous Puget Sound transit riders.

Please click on the image of the product that you would be the most likely to buy. Many of the items will appear multiple times; in each instance, just vote on your favorite of the images that are displayed.
